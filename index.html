Awards & Recognition by ACBSP

ACBSP recognizes and awards faculty, students, and members of the business community who go above and beyond in the pursuit of business education. The International Teaching Excellence Award focuses on teaching in the classroom. The Best of Regions is a competition between faculty presentations from ACBSP's ten geographical regions. The Student Showcase gives students, from all degree levels, at ACBSP member schools the opportunity to present their academic research projects. The Student Leadership Award recognizes students who exemplify the best qualities of leadership. Lastly, The ACBSP Educational Impact Award recognizes the most effective and influential educational initiatives by an organization in the host city of the ACBSP Annual Conference, in support of learning and education in primary, secondary, and post-secondary institutions and the community.
